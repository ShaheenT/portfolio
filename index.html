About Me

I’m an AI-driven Quality Assurance Engineer passionate about accelerating software testing with intelligence, precision, and speed. At EntrprnriX Ai, I leverage AI-powered frameworks—Testim, Mabl, Functionize, Applitools, and TestSigma—alongside tools like Playwright, Selenium, Cypress, Appium, and Postman to design end-to-end QA strategies. My work blends traditional QA rigor with AI-enhanced automation, self-healing tests, and predictive defect analytics, ensuring software is functional, secure, scalable, and user-friendly. I specialize in helping startups and high-growth tech companies: Reduce time-to-market with AI-generated test scripts and intelligent automation. Optimize QA workflows to deliver high-quality releases with lean teams. Provide predictive insights to prioritize risk and improve product reliability. By combining AI-driven automation, predictive analytics, and deep QA expertise, I help companies build secure, scalable, and market-ready applications faster—without compromising quality.
